PI8 Gamification System: Mobile/Web Application

Pi8 (Project Innov8) is a student club in Temasek Polytechnic which offers events, trainings and projects to students. The PI8 gamification system encourages students to join trainings, projects and events without heavily relying on motivators like SEAL points and monetary incentives. The system also encourages students to self-start projects. This is done by gamifying and rewarding, participation in trainings, projects and events, as well as self-starting projects.
